If token has expired, follow these steps to re-init test app.

1. Go to https://partners.dnaspaces.io/.
2. Click on "App Activation Sandbox".
3. Click on the 'IH-TEST2' app 'gear' in the top right corner.
4. Click on "Renew".
5. Click on "Generate Activation Key".
6. Copy the generated key.
7. Input the copied key when prompted while running start.py or index.py.
